Journey to PyColorado

* All of us made a journey to get to PyColorado. * Some walked over from Union Station, * Many of our community here on the front range came to join us this weekend from Boulder, Fort Collins, or Colorado Springs * Some came from further - Minturn, Nederland, Carbondale * And still some from even further - Illinois, New York, Ohio, DC, Florida, California * I'd like to share some of the journey that brought PyColorado to all of you.

Packing the Wagon

Why no regional conference in Colorado?
  • Fun place to visit
  • 2 tech hubs
  • Countless tech communities
  • Lots of Python Users
* Frank and I at PyCon 2018. * 300 days of sunshine * 58 14' foot peaks * 300+ pieces of public art * 140 craft breweries
No 1 dominant community, not much overlap. Lots of unique perspectives across each of the communities.
